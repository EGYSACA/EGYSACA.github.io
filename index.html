About Me

My name is Nadia Gouda. I am an educator and engineer with experience in Electrical and Computer Engineering, programming, networking, and web development. I am a faculty member at NSCC eCampus where I teach IT Programming and Networking courses. I have always believed that learning becomes powerful when it is simple and accessible to everyone.

I enjoy helping students grow, guiding beginners to understand complex ideas in easy steps, and creating learning materials that make every student feel confident. I am passionate about technology, education, creativity, and building new ideas that help people learn and explore their potential.
